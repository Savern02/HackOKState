What VolunTold Does

VolunTold creates and fosters a community of dedicated volunteers through innovative features:

Discover Opportunities

Explore volunteer opportunities in your area through our Discover tab, powered by Google Gemini. Our community-driven approach allows users to share and discover local volunteer opportunities, creating a growing network of possibilities.

Create & Connect

Organizations can create and manage volunteer opportunities directly through our platform. This feature enables quick mobilization of volunteers, especially crucial during crisis situations, ensuring help reaches where it's needed most.
